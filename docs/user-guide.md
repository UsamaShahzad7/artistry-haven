# The Artistry Haven — User Guide

A simple guide for using the website. No tech experience needed.

---

## For Customers

### Browsing Products

1. **Open the website** — You'll land on the home page with featured pieces at the top.
2. **Scroll down to see everything** — Keep scrolling to find the full collection. You can also tap **Shop** in the top menu to go straight to all products.
3. **Filter by type (optional)** — On the shop page, tap **Keychains & Charms** or **Canvas Paintings** to see only that type. Tap **All** to go back to everything.

> **Tip:** Products marked *Sold Out* are no longer available. You can DM on Instagram to ask when they'll be restocked.

---

### Viewing a Product

1. **Click or tap on any product** — A full product page opens with all the photos, the price, and a description.
2. **Swipe through the photos** — If there are multiple photos, use the arrow buttons on the image to go left or right. The dots at the bottom show how many photos there are.
3. **Go back to shopping** — Tap **← Back to Shop** at the top left to return to the collection.

---

### Adding to Your Cart

1. **Tap "Add to Cart"** — On any product, tap the **Add to Cart** button (either from the shop listing or the product page).
2. **Open your cart** — Tap the bag icon 🛍 in the top-right corner. A panel slides open from the right showing everything you've added.
3. **Adjust quantities if needed** — Use the **+** and **−** buttons next to each item. To remove something, tap the **×** next to it.

> **Tip:** Your cart is saved in your browser. If you close the page by mistake, just reopen the website — your items will still be there.

---

### Placing an Order

1. **Open your cart and check it** — Make sure the product names, quantities, and total price are correct.
2. **Tap "Order via WhatsApp"** — This button is inside the cart panel. It opens WhatsApp with your order already written out — you don't need to type anything.
3. **Send the message** — Press send in WhatsApp. The shop will reply to confirm your order and arrange payment and delivery.

> **Note:** WhatsApp must be installed on your phone or open in your browser. If it doesn't open automatically, you can also DM the shop directly on Instagram: **@artistryhaven._**

## For the Shop Owner (Admin)

### Logging In

1. **Go to the admin page** — Add `/admin` to the end of the website address in your browser (e.g. `yoursite.com/admin`).
2. **Enter your 6-digit PIN** — Type your secret PIN and tap **Login**. If you forget the PIN, contact your developer.
3. **You're in the dashboard** — You'll see links to manage Products and Testimonials. To log out, use the **Sign Out** button at the top.

---

### Adding a New Product

1. **Go to Products → New Product** — From the dashboard, click **Products**, then tap **+ New Product**.
2. **Fill in the details** — Write the product name, price (numbers only, no "Rs."), choose **Keychain** or **Canvas**, and add a description.
3. **Upload photos** — Click the dashed upload box and choose one or more photos from your phone or computer. You can add several — customers will be able to swipe through them.
4. **Mark as Featured (optional)** — Tick **Featured** if you want this product shown in the spotlight section on the home page.
5. **Save** — Tap **Save Product**. The product appears on the website within about a minute.

> **Note:** To *edit* an existing product, go to Products and click on it. To mark it as *Sold Out*, uncheck the "In Stock" box and save.

---

### Managing Testimonials

1. **Go to Testimonials** — From the dashboard, click **Testimonials** to see all reviews you've added.
2. **Add a new review** — Tap **+ New Testimonial**. Write the customer's name, their review, and pick a star rating (1–5). Tick **Published** so it shows on the site.
3. **Hide a review** — Click on any testimonial and uncheck **Published**, then save. It disappears from the site but isn't deleted.

> **Tip:** Reviews show up on the home page and on every product's detail page — even a few genuine reviews make a big difference.
